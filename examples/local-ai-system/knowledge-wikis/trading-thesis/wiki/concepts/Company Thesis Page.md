# Company Thesis Page

## Purpose

公司 thesis 页用于记录单个标的的长期判断框架，而不是临时观点堆积。

## Suggested Sections

- Thesis statement
- Business / narrative summary
- Core catalysts
- Risk triggers
- Valuation or expectation gap
- Status and latest update
