# Catalyst Tracking

## Definition

Catalyst tracking 用于记录可能推动 thesis 演进的事件，并判断这些事件是否仍支持原始判断。

## Common Catalyst Types

- Earnings
- Product release
- Policy event
- Macro cycle turn
- Capital flow change
- Sector re-rating
