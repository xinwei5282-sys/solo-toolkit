# Risk Triggers

## Definition

Risk trigger 是导致交易 thesis 需要降级、重估或终止的信号。

## Typical Trigger Types

- Price structure failure
- Volume / liquidity deterioration
- Earnings or guidance miss
- Policy change
- Sector narrative reversal
- Original catalyst delayed or invalidated
